Timestamp microservice.

Type a Unix timestamp (seconds) or 'natural language form of the date' (MMM DD, YYYY e.g. Dec 25, 2015 or December 25, 2015) after the current URL /yourInputHere.

OR

Type /api/timestamp/yourInputHere for a Unix timestamp (milliseconds) or date strings compliant with ISO-8601 (YYYY-MM-DD e.g. 2015-12-25)
